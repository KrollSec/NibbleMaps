# NibbleMaps Content & Marketing Strategy

Comprehensive guide to optimizing your website content and driving conversions.

## Content Philosophy

Your website copy should follow these principles:

1. **Clear over Clever** - "Track Your Food Journey" beats "Your Culinary Compass"
2. **Benefits over Features** - "Never forget an amazing meal" vs "GPS-enabled location tracking"
3. **Active Voice** - "Discover new spots" not "New spots can be discovered"
4. **Conversational Tone** - Write like you're talking to a friend about food
5. **Scannable Format** - Short paragraphs, bullet points, clear headers

## Content Testing Framework

### What to Test

**Headlines:**
- Hero headline
- Feature headlines
- CTA button text

**Copy Length:**
- Short vs. long feature descriptions
- Brief vs. detailed testimonials

**Visuals:**
- Different app screenshots
- Real vs. illustrated graphics
- With/without user photos

**Social Proof:**
- Placement of testimonials
- Number of testimonials shown
- Stats vs. reviews

**CTAs:**
- Button color (red vs. blue vs. green)
- Button size
- Button text
- Button placement

### Testing Tools

**Free:**
- Google Optimize (discontinued - use GA4 experiments)
- Microsoft Clarity (heatmaps)
- Hotjar (limited free plan)

**Paid:**
- Optimizely
- VWO
- Convert

### Minimum Sample Size

Don't make decisions until you have:
- At least 1,000 visitors per variation
- Statistical significance (95% confidence)
- At least 2 weeks of data

## Voice & Tone Guidelines

### Brand Voice

**NibbleMaps is:**
- Friendly and approachable
- Enthusiastic about food
- Community-oriented
- Design-conscious
- Encouraging and positive

**NibbleMaps is NOT:**
- Pretentious or elitist
- Technical or jargon-heavy
- Corporate or cold
- Overly casual or unprofessional

### Tone Examples

**Good:**
"Turn every meal into a memory with your personal food passport."

**Bad:**
"Leverage our geo-location technology to capture dining experiences."

**Good:**
"Food is better when shared. Connect with friends and discover their favorite spots."

**Bad:**
"Utilize our social networking features to aggregate user-generated restaurant recommendations."

**Good:**
"Ready to start your food journey? Download NibbleMaps today."

**Bad:**
"Download our application to commence utilization of premium features."

### Messaging Pillars

1. **Discovery** - Find new places through friends
2. **Memory** - Never forget great meals
3. **Connection** - Share experiences with community
4. **Visual** - Beautiful, intuitive design
5. **Personal** - Your journey, your way

Every piece of content should reinforce at least one pillar.

**Engagement:**

- Pages per session (target: 1.5+)

**Conversion:**
- CTA click rate (target: 5-10%)

**SEO:**
- Organic traffic growth (target: 20% MoM)
- Keyword rankings (target: top 10 for primary keywords)
